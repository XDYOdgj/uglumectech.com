Megaflint Keyless Combination Door Lock 304 Stainless Steel

USD38.84
A durable, keyless combination door lock suitable for left-handed doors opening inward. Features a digital keypad with customizable 4-8 digit combinations and a lifetime mechanical warranty for reliable security.
  • Description
  • Reviews

  This Megaflint keyless combination door lock offers a modern, secure, and convenient way to manage access to your property. With a digital code keypad, you can grant entry without the need for physical keys. The lock features a 304 stainless steel body, ensuring long-lasting durability and resistance to rust and weather. The 4-8 digit combination system provides customizable security settings, granting over 8,000 unique options to suit your needs. It is designed specifically for left-handed doors that open inward, with the handle needing to be pushed up to reset the keypad.
  Whether you're looking to secure your home or business, this Megaflint combination door lock delivers reliable and easy-to-use functionality. Ideal for offices, workshops, storerooms, and other spaces where restricted but convenient access is required, it combines security with flexibility. The anti-friction spring latch ensures smooth operation, and it is compatible with both 2-3/8" (60mm) and 2-3/4" (70mm) backset distances. The lock's lack of automatic locking function makes it suitable for users who prefer manual control.
