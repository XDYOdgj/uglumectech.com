  The MrArm Keyed Entry Privacy Indicator Lock is the perfect solution for boosting privacy and security in restrooms. Its keyed design ensures only authorized individuals can access the room, making it ideal for use in commercial and public spaces such as restaurants, hospitals, schools, and laboratories. The lock features a durable, colorful indicator wheel that clearly shows whether the restroom is in use or vacant, eliminating the need for old-fashioned stickers that tend to peel or fade. With a sleek satin chrome handle, the lock blends well with modern bathroom decor without compromising on security.
  Designed for both left and right-handed doors, the MrArm lock offers versatility and ease of installation. Its drop-in style allows quick setup on doors with thickness ranging from 1 3/8" to 1 3/4", and the adjustable backset accommodates 60mm or 70mm pre-drilled knob holes. The exterior anti-lockout safety notch prevents accidental lock-ins and allows for emergency access with a coin or flat head screwdriver. This commercial-grade lock is built to last with 200,000 opening and closing cycles tested, ensuring long-term reliability and performance in any environment.
